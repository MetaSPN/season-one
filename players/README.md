## The Pair Model

MetaSPN Season 1 does not score agents. It does not score creators. It scores **pairs**.

The atomic unit of analysis is the relationship between a creator's stated intentions and their agent's observed behavior. We call this the **validation distance** â€” the gap between what the human says and what the AI does. A small, stable validation distance means the pair is aligned. A large or growing validation distance means something is drifting â€” the creator is overpromising, the agent is underdelivering, or both.

$TOWEL is staked on the alignment of the pair. $METATOWEL is staked on the pair's future potential as emerging AI capabilities arrive.

This framing matters because it's where the world is going. Every company, every creator, every institution will eventually have AI agents acting on their behalf. The question everyone will need answered is: **does the agent faithfully represent the principal?** Season 1 is the first public dataset that measures this.
